Why I love it

Songs is an intimate acoustic album by Adrianne Lenker, recorded alone in a quiet cabin, giving it a raw and honest sound. The album explores love, vulnerability, loss, and healing through soft vocals and gentle guitar melodies. It creates a calm, emotional atmosphere perfect for late-night listening and self-reflection.
