Why I love it

The Dark Side of the Moon is a legendary progressive rock album by Pink Floyd, known for its rich sound design and conceptual storytelling. The album explores themes of time, mental health, greed, death, and the pressure of modern life. Its seamless track transitions and emotional depth make it one of the most iconic albums in music history.
