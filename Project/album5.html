Why I love it

England Made Me is a dark and minimalistic debut album by Black Box Recorder, released in 1998. The album explores loneliness, depression, social pressure, and the hidden sadness of everyday life in England. Its cold electronic sound and emotionless vocals create a haunting and unforgettable atmosphere.
