Why I love it

Grace is the debut album by Jeff Buckley, blending rock, folk, and soul into a deeply emotional sound. The album explores themes of love, longing, heartbreak, and spiritual searching through powerful vocals and poetic lyrics. It is widely considered one of the most influential and timeless albums of the 1990s.
